INNER CHILD FACILITATOR TRAINING (ICFT)

WHAT IT IS:

ICFT is a 6-month immersive certification program designed to train professionals in facilitating transformative inner child healing for their clients.

HOW IT’S DELIVERED:

Live Zoom training sessions, recorded resources, customizable scripts, and hands-on practice with live demos and personalized feedback.

WHO IT’S FOR:

Therapists, coaches, mentors, and leaders seeking to expand their skillset with advanced tools to guide clients through emotional healing and integration. Ideal for seasoned and new practitioners ready to elevate their offerings or individuals entering advanced facilitation.

ITS LENGTH:

6 months, including 40-50 hours of live training and over 20 hours of hands-on practice with feedback.

HIGH-LEVEL DESCRIPTION:

This program equips you with cutting-edge somatic and subconscious tools to create safety, transformation, and integration for your clients. You’ll gain leadership skills, customizable scripts, and personal healing experiences, enabling you to facilitate life-changing inner child work with confidence and helping you to feel empowered to hold safe and transformative spaces for clients. You’ll also receive a certification to enhance your professional credibility, and be given access to the Wonder Child course to heal your own inner child through first hand experience.

MAJOR OUTCOMES:
  • Gain a comprehensive understanding of inner child work, including how to identify and heal childhood wounds.
  • Develop advanced skills in creating transformation for clients through subconscious and somatic approaches to inner child work.
  • Master the ability to create your own sessions, impactful workshops, retreats, and more.
  • Walk away with a certification and advanced tools that elevate your professional offerings.
